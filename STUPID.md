# Tender participation requirements — SI „Protecția Socială" CNAS 2026–2028

Source: `tor/TOR.md` lines 18900–19800 (the Anunț de Participare + Anexa 2 +
the 11-position key-personnel table). Reference: Ordinul MF 115/2021
(Documentația standard) + Legea 131/2015 privind achizițiile publice.

## 1. Contract basics

- **CPV:** `72000000-5` — Servicii IT (consultanță, dezvoltare software,
  internet, asistență).
- **Estimated value:** **37,500,000 MDL** fără TVA, 2026–2028.
- **Term of contract:** through **31.12.2028**.
- **Contracting authority:** Casa Națională de Asigurări Sociale (CNAS).
- **Submission channel:** electronic only via **SIA RSAP**.
- **Language:** Romanian (state language).
- **Bid validity:** **90 days** from submission deadline.
- **Award criterion:** best price-quality ratio (cel mai bun raport
  calitate-preț), per Anexa 2.
- **Currency:** Moldovan Lei (MDL).
- **Procedure ID:** `c7a17a99-ff95-4e70-b430-52dcacc2fba7`.
- **Subject to WTO GPA:** Yes — open to foreign bidders.

## 2. Eligibility — mandatory exclusions

Bidder excluded if any of these apply:

- Convicted in last 5 years for organised crime, corruption, fraud,
  money-laundering, terrorism, terrorism financing, child labour, human
  trafficking (point 22).
- Falls under situations in **art. 19(2)/(3) and art. 16(6) of
  Legea 131/2015** (point 23).
- Currently in **insolvency** by court ruling.
- Unpaid taxes / social-insurance contributions in Moldova or country
  of establishment (verified via MConnect / data.gov.md).
- Mandatory **declaration of beneficial owners** (Anexa 1) — none of
  them convicted of the above offences in last 5 years.

## 5. Economic & financial capacity (mandatory minimum)

- **Minimum 3 years** similar-service delivery experience.
- **Minimum annual turnover** in similar activities:
  **10,000,000 MDL per year for each of the last 3 years**.
- Evidence: declaration in DUAE → contracts + invoices + acceptance
  acts on request.
- In a consortium: aggregated cumulatively across members.

## 6. Technical & professional capacity (mandatory minimum)

- **Minimum 3 ICT projects of similar complexity, successfully
  implemented in the last 15 years.**
  - "Similar complexity" = comparable scope of analysis / design /
    development / integration / testing / implementation / maintenance
    of an information system.
  - For each project: name, beneficiary, domain (social-security /
    social-insurance), period, contract value, technical complexity,
    role (sole contractor / consortium leader / member), completion
    status.
- Confirmation via: copies of contracts/annexes, recepție finală
  minutes, certificates of good execution, beneficiary recommendations.

## 8. Evaluation weights (Anexa 2)

| Factor | Weight |
|---|---|
| Price | **40 %** |
| Experience in similar projects | **30 %** |
| Technical & professional capacity | **20 %** |
| Quality of technical proposal & implementation plan | **10 %** |

Sub-scoring is granular (e.g. project complexity broken into
functional / technical / organisational dimensions, each scored
0 / 3 / 7 / 10).

## 9. Quality / management standards expected

- Quality assurance per **ISO 9001** equivalents (point 34).
- Environmental certification per **EMAS / ISO 14001-family** (point 36).
- The TOR cites **SM EN ISO/IEC 27001:2017** for information-security
  and **ISO 20000 / ITIL** for service management — held as the
  practice baseline.

- **Appeal body:** Agenția Națională pentru Soluționarea Contestațiilor

## 11. What disqualifies a bid immediately

- Late submission (rejected on the spot).
- Missing mandatory document from §3 above.
- Bid bond not constituted / wrong amount / wrong form.
- DUAE not on the official standard form (Ord. MF 72/2020).
- Failure to demonstrate any of the mandatory minimums in §5–7 above.
- Listed on Lista de interdicție of the Agenția Achiziții Publice.
- Beneficial-owner declaration missing or false.
- Conflict of interest (drafted the TOR; involved in bid evaluation).

---

**Bottom line — to even open the bid you need:** ≥ 10 M MDL/year
turnover × 3 years in similar work, ≥ 3 successful similar ICT
projects in the last 15 years, all 11 key roles staffed with people
meeting the specific years/certifications, and the full
mandatory-document set with electronic signatures and a bank-backed
1 % bid bond. Then you compete on the 40 / 30 / 20 / 10 scoring grid.
