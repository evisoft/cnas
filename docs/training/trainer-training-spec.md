# Trainer training specification (≥4 trainers, ≥56 hours)

> Anchored to TOR ID(s): R2442 (UTD 008, Milestone M5). Companion to
> R2440 ([`training-plan.md`](training-plan.md)). Iteration 103.

## 1. Purpose

Define the train-the-trainer programme so CNAS has at least four
internal trainers able to deliver the end-user curriculum (R2443)
unaided after supplier handover. Locks in the TOR-mandated
quantitative thresholds: ≥ 4 trainers, ≥ 56 instruction hours each,
delivered in Romanian and Russian.

## 2. Audience / stakeholders

- CNAS-designated trainers (≥ 4 named persons).
- Supplier training lead and pedagogy reviewer.
- CNAS HR (records keeper).
- Joint acceptance committee for UTD 008.

## 4. Acceptance criteria

- ≥ 4 named CNAS trainers enrolled and attended ≥ 56 hours each.
- Each trainer passes a teach-back evaluation (≥ 75%).
- Each trainer demonstrates bilingual RO + RU delivery on at least one
  module.
- Attendance log + teach-back scorecards signed per learner.
- Bilingual material library handed over and archived.
- Sign-off entered in the Acceptance Protocol row "UTD 008 / R2442".

## 5. Status / open gaps

- Slide decks, workbooks, and assessment item bank: pending (parent
